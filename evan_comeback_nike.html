// 05 — Gut Health Protocol
BUILD YOUR
MICROBIOME
🦠

Probiotic Foods Daily

Curd/yogurt (unsweetened), homemade pickles (no vinegar), idli/dosa batter, fermented foods. These seed your gut with Lactobacillus and Bifidobacterium — your immunity and mood bacteria.

🌿

Prebiotic Foods Daily

Oats (beta-glucan), raw garlic (crush and wait 10 min), onion, green banana, lentils, cooked-then-cooled rice. These FEED your good bacteria — equally critical as probiotics.

🌾

30 Plant Foods / Week

Sounds intimidating — it's not. Oats + 5 veggies + 5 fruits + 3 nuts + 2 seeds + lentils + herbs = 16+ easily. Rotate daily. Gut diversity = immune strength = lower inflammation = better skin and hair.

💧

3–3.5 Litres Water Daily

Gut motility depends on hydration. Add sabja/basil seeds to water — they form a mucilaginous coating that soothes the gut lining. Morning ACV supports stomach acid production.

🚫

Gut Killers — Avoid

Refined sugar, processed foods, alcohol, artificial sweeteners (destroy Lactobacillus). These wipe out microbiome diversity within 48 hours. Your Man Matters meds need a healthy gut to absorb properly too.
